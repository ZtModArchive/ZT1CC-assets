To modify a tank, click the tank platform to view the exhibit information for that tank. The first tab,  Modify Tank, contains all the controls you need to modify your tank. Clicking Drain/Fill tank empties a full tank or fills an empty one. You can also change the water salinity by draining the tank and then clicking either Salt Water or Fresh Water to fill the tank with the specified type of water. Clicking Adjust Wall Up or Adjust Wall Down will change the height of the tank walls. To change the elevation of the entire tank, click Recess Tank or Expose Tank. The cost of each tank adjustment is displayed next to each control.
