To modify a tank, click the tank platform to view the exhibit information for that tank. The first tab, Tank Modification, contains all the controls you need to modify your tank. You can change the water salinity by clicking either Salt Water or Fresh Water. Clicking Adjust Wall Up or Adjust Wall Down will change the height of the tank walls. To change the elevation of the entire tank, click Recess Tank or Expose Tank. You can also click Drain/Fill to empty a full tank or fill an empty tank. The cost of each tank adjustment is displayed on this tab.
